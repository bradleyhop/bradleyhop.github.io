Give New Life To Old Games

Love your old Game Boy but find that it is too difficult to see? Too blury? Have to hold at just the right angle in the light? Sure, you could play your original Game Boy games on other hardware, but the best experience is always on original hardware!

Give your Game Boy a new life!

  • Fully clean unit, replace screen with new glass replica.
  • Install backlight and circuitry to double invert video signal to improve contrast and see your Game Boy like never before.
  • Replace aging off-the-shelf components to protect and improve effeciancy of the circuitry.

BACKLIGHT ONLY

This option includes this installation of only the backlight and bivert modules.

BACKLIGHT and RESTORATION

This option includes the installation of the backlight and bivert module in addition to:

  • Replacement of all electrolytic capacitors
  • Cleaning of battery contacts, pots, and cartridge slot
  • Replace worn rubber button membranes
